These Are My Top Rated Movies

Top Boy

Top Boy, the electrifying British crime drama, masterminded by the brilliant Ronan Bennett, unfolds in the heart of Hackney's Summerhouse estate.
It's a thrilling narrative of the gripping world of drug lords Dushane (Ashley Walters) and Sully (Kane Robinson), alongside a host of characters entangled in the web of London's gritty drug trade and gang warfare.

Titanic

James Cameron's 'Titanic' sails us into an exhilarating world of epic romance, adventure, and heart-pounding action.
It unfolds amidst the ill-fated maiden voyage of the R.M.S. Titanic, a marvel of the White Star Line and, in its time, the grandest, most colossal creation in motion.
